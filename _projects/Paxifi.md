  <h2 class="content-h2 with-bottom-line">Paxifi</h2>

#### Mission

Bringing innovation to a long established environment can sometimes be an impossible task. However technology is often the catalyst to make this happen. In this case the humble taxi journey and mobile evolution respectively.

#### Concept

Paxifi is the convenience store on wheels; allowing taxi drivers to offer their passengers everyday essentials from; umbrellas to mineral water, lipstick to condoms.

The Paxify platform creates an additional income stream for drivers, as well as turning them into lifesavers for their passengers.

Drivers manage inventory and track sales via a native app, whilst passengers browse and transact through a simple mobile site.

#### Results

Beta-Launched in the USA and in the UK in spring 2015, with more countries coming onboard throughout 2015.
