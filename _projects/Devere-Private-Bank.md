  <h2 class="content-h2 with-bottom-line">Devere Private Bank</h2>

#### Mission

Devere Group are an innovative financial services Client with great ambition. In 2010, they tasked us with launching their app the same day iPad became available.

#### Concept

We created a dashboard app, that allowed users to view their portfolios on the go. Push notifications were leveraged to help alert users on whether to make a timely buy or sell.

#### Results

The app was released just in time for the worldwide iPad launch; which created a lot of positive PR in the category. Devere customers, as well as DeVere consultants are now using the app everyday to stay on top of the funds market.
